# Snowplan — Support

Snowplan is an offline debt payoff planner. Add your debts, set a monthly budget,
and see exactly when you'll be debt-free using the snowball or avalanche method.
**Pay once, no subscription, no account, no bank linking.**

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I add a debt?**
Tap the **+** on the Debts tab and enter the name, balance, APR, and minimum
payment from your latest statement. Use 0% APR for interest-free debts.

**What's the difference between Snowball and Avalanche?**
- **Snowball** pays the smallest balance first — quick wins keep you motivated.
- **Avalanche** pays the highest APR first — costs the least total interest.
Set your strategy on the Plan tab; Pro unlocks a side-by-side comparison.

**How do I log a payment?**
Open a debt and tap **Log Payment**. The balance and your progress update
automatically.

**The free version limits me.**
Free includes 3 debts and a 12-month schedule. **Pro** (one-time purchase)
unlocks unlimited debts, the full payoff schedule, strategy comparison, and a
monthly payment reminder.

**I can't restore my purchase.**
Go to Settings and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you're signed in with the
same Apple ID used for the purchase.

**Is my data private?**
Yes — everything stays on your device. The app has no account and no servers.
