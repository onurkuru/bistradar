# Snowplan — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 11, 2026**

Snowplan ("the app") is built to be completely private. **We collect nothing.**

## Data we collect

**None.** Snowplan has no account, no login, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking.

- Your debts, payments, budget, and plan are stored **only on your device**
  (Apple SwiftData). They are never transmitted to us or anyone else.
- The app has **no backend server** and makes no network requests with your data.
- The app works fully **offline**.

## Purchases

The optional one-time "Pro" purchase is handled entirely by **Apple StoreKit**.
We never see your payment information. Apple processes the transaction; we only
receive a yes/no entitlement that unlocks Pro features on your device.

## Notifications

If you enable the optional payment reminder, the notification is scheduled
**locally on your device**. No reminder data leaves your device and there is no
push server.

## Data export

The optional "Export data" feature creates a JSON file from your own data and
hands it to the iOS share sheet for **you** to save or send. We are not involved.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from anyone.

## Not financial advice

Snowplan is a planning and calculation tool. It does not provide financial or
investment advice. Your financial decisions are your own.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always
published on this page.
